Everything Anya wanted

Every single thing below started the same way — Anya wanted something. Here's what she's asked for so far, and what she's asking for next.

🕰️ The story so far

What Anya's asked for

One giggle, one ask, one sparkle at a time.

June 25, 2026
🌱 Anya's mom wanted to make something for Anya at a hackathon
Anya wanted a screen with no ads and no scary pop-ups — somewhere that felt like it was hers. That's SparklePop's very first version: a few taps, some colors, a whole lot of sparkle.
June 26, 2026
🏡 Anya wants it all in one place
Anya wanted to jump between painting and her pet without getting lost — so she got a home screen to choose from, plus new worlds to explore so she'd never get bored.
June 26, 2026
🦄 Anya wants a pet of her own
Anya wanted a unicorn to look after — so she got one she could name, feed, and watch grow.
June 27, 2026
🎨 Anya wants to spray paint the rainbow
Anya wanted to spray paint like a real artist — so a spray tool and a rainbow color mode were born. Every painting since has looked like actual magic.
June 27, 2026
🥚 Anya wants surprises
Anya kept asking "what happens if I tap this?" — so the world filled up with things to discover: surprise eggs, snow on the moon, a dancing unicorn, a secret puppy party, and a tiny baby companion.
June 27–28, 2026
👑 Anya wants to dress up her unicorn
Anya wanted her unicorn to look exactly how she imagined it — glasses, jewels, a fluffy coat, new horns, even a castle to visit.
June 28, 2026
🎁 Anya wants surprises that feel special
The rewards were coming too fast to really enjoy — so we slowed them down and spaced them out. Every surprise gets its own moment now.
June 29, 2026
🦋 Anya wants her butterflies colorful
Anya asked why the butterflies were always the same color — now they flutter by in random colors. (And her iPad finally has sound, too.)
July 19, 2026
💌 Anya wants people to know where this came from
Anya wanted her friends to know where SparklePop came from — so now there's a page telling every family it was made by a mom, for her daughter.
July 19, 2026
🐛 Anya wants buttons to actually work
Anya tapped the "Our Story" badge and asked, "Mumma, what's this button?" It wasn't one — yet. Now it is: tap the heart on the story page and watch the sparkles fly.
July 19, 2026
📸 Anya wants the photo to do something
Anya poked our photo on the story page expecting it to do something — so now it does. Tap it for a little wiggle, a burst of confetti, and a hello from us.
July 19, 2026
🎙️ Anya wants to make stickers with her voice
Anya asked, "how will we make a sticker?" — and wanted to do it just by talking. So now she can! She taps the big mic, says something magical like "a rainbow unicorn with stars," and SparklePop turns her words into a sticker while a gentle voice cheers her on. No typing, no reading. On a device without a mic, she can tap a picture or hit "Surprise me!"
July 19, 2026
⏰💡 Anya's mom wants play time to teach something too
Anya's mom wanted a gentle pause after 15 minutes, and a little learning mixed into playtime — so now a friendly unicorn checks in every 15 minutes, and every time an egg hatches, SparklePop reads Anya a fun fact out loud.
