💖 Our story

Hi, I'm Giti — a mom who made this for her daughter.

SparklePop started as something small: a few taps and swirls of color on a screen, built one evening so my daughter would have somewhere gentle and joyful to play — no ads popping up, no pressure to buy anything, nothing scary. Just sparkles, unicorns, and a little world that was entirely hers.

She loved it so much that I kept building. A pet to name and take care of. New colors to paint with. Little surprises to discover. Watching her giggle at a screen full of stars is honestly the whole reason this exists.

Then I thought — if it makes her this happy, maybe it can do the same for other toddlers too. So I'm sharing it, for free, with your family.

With love,
Giti 🦄
